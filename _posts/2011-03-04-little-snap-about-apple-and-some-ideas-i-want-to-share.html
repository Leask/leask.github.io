Look at the picture.
It is a screenshot I made from iPad 2 conference, March 2nd just past.

This image has been exhibited many times by Steve Jobs.
“It’s in Apple’s DNA that technology is not enough,” he noted.

more below:
"It’s tech married with the liberal arts and the humanities. Nowhere is that more true than in the post-PC products. Our competitors are looking at this like it’s the next PC market. That is not the right approach to this. These are post-PC devices that need to be easier to use than a PC, more intuitive. and the software, hardware, and applications need to intertwine in an even more seamless way than on a PC. We think we’re on track with this."

I think that's why Apple can make great products again and again.
Also it's the reason why I love Apple and what we need to think about as a full time developer:
To make cool things full of liberal arts and humanities to everyone with powerful tech.

It's just the beginning of the great time about cloud computing, mobile internet and some other cool stuffs.
And it's the right timing for us to grow our self to catch more opportunities.

When and where to begin?
It began already, from our everyday's working.

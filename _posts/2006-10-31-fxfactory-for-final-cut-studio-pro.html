A revolutionary visual effects package for Final Cut Studio, offering over 100 GPU-accelerated plug-ins based on the FxPlug architecture for the highest performance inside Final Cut Pro and Motion.



FxFactory Pro is the only software that lets you create and modify your own visual effects plug-ins for Final Cut Pro and Motion without writing a single line of code.



By leveraging modern graphics technology such as Core Image and Quartz Composer, your plug-ins will be high-performance, first-class FxPlug citizens.
